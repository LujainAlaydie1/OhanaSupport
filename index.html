Privacy Policy

Data Management & Firebase

The app uses **Google Firebase** to host and synchronize the game library, descriptions, and real-time updates. This ensures that all team members see the most current game data during events.

Personal Information

Ohana does not require a user account and does not collect, store, or share any personal identifiable information (PII) such as names, emails, or phone numbers in Firebase or elsewhere.

Leaderboard & Analytics

Game scores and leaderboard rankings are managed through Firebase to provide a consistent experience for the team. This data is used strictly for internal event tracking and is not used for advertising or third-party tracking.

External Content

Game images are fetched from public online sources via Firebase links. These requests are strictly for visual display within the app.
